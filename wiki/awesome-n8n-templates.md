# awesome-n8n-templates

A curated collection of 280+ free, ready-to-import [[n8n]] workflow templates, maintained by [enescingoz](https://github.com/enescingoz/awesome-n8n-templates). This is the largest open-source repository of n8n automation templates on GitHub.

## Overview

The repository organizes templates into 18 categories covering a wide range of platforms and use cases:

- **Communication & Email**: Gmail, Outlook, IMAP
- **Messaging**: Telegram, Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, LINE
- **Office & Productivity**: Google Drive, Google Sheets, Notion, Obsidian, Todoist
- **Content Management**: WordPress, Confluence, Strapi
- **CRM & Sales**: Airtable, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Bitrix24
- **AI & LLMs**: OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, DeepSeek R1, Mistral AI, Ollama, LangChain, Perplexity, Hugging Face
- **Databases & Vector Stores**: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, SQLite, Supabase, Pinecone, Qdrant, Elasticsearch
- **Social Media**: Instagram, Twitter/X, Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn, Pinterest, TikTok
- **DevOps & Security**: SSH, Docker Compose, Qualys, SIEM enrichment, Venafi
- **Other**: Zoom, Spotify, Cal.com, Twilio, Strapi, ERPNext, Printify, Apify

The repository also includes localized READMEs in 13 languages and usage statistics tracked via star-history.
